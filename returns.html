Our Policy
Your Purchase is Protected

At Nuvox, we are committed to your satisfaction. If you receive a product with a verified factory defect or that significantly differs from what was described, you may return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or replacement.

We take every return request seriously and aim to resolve all issues fairly and promptly.

Eligibility
What Can & Cannot Be Returned
✓ Eligible for Return
  • Factory defects present on arrival
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Item significantly different from description
  • Dead-on-arrival (DOA) devices
  • Item arrives visibly damaged from transit
✗ Not Eligible for Return
  • Physical damage caused by customer
  • Items returned after 7 days
  • Products without original packaging
  • Damage from incorrect installation
  • Electrical damage from unstable power supply
  • Change of mind after use
How to Return
The Return Process
1
Contact Us Within 7 Days
Email us at emmaire7@gmail.com with your order reference number, a description of the issue, and clear photos of the defect.
2
Await Confirmation
We will review your request and respond within 24 hours. If approved, we will provide return instructions and a return address.
3
Return the Item
Send the item back in its original packaging with all accessories included. The item must be in the same condition as received.
4
Refund or Replacement
Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund to the original payment method or dispatch a replacement within 3–5 business days.
Important Notes
Please Read Carefully
Return shipping costs are covered by Nuvox for eligible defective items. Customer bears the cost for non-eligible returns.
Refunds are processed to the original payment method (Paystack or Flutterwave) and may take 3–7 business days to reflect in your account.
All returns must be approved before sending. Do not send items back without prior confirmation from our team.
Items must be returned with all original accessories, manuals, and packaging materials.
