Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Payments
How do I place an order on Nuvox?
+

Simply browse our store, add your chosen product to cart, and click checkout. Fill in your delivery details and choose either Paystack or Flutterwave to complete your payment securely. You will receive an order confirmation with your reference number.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept payments via Paystack and Flutterwave, which support debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and USSD. All transactions are encrypted and secure. We do not store your card details.

Is it safe to pay on Nuvox?
+

Yes, absolutely. All payments on Nuvox are processed by Paystack and Flutterwave — Nigeria's most trusted payment gateways. Your card details go directly to them and are never stored on our servers. Every transaction is verified server-side before your order is confirmed.

I was charged but didn't receive an order confirmation. What should I do?
+

Don't worry — contact us immediately at emmaire7@gmail.com with your payment reference number (from your bank or Paystack/Flutterwave). We will verify the transaction and confirm your order within 24 hours.

Can I cancel my order after payment?
+

Orders can only be cancelled before they are dispatched. Contact us as soon as possible at emmaire7@gmail.com with your order reference. Once an item has been shipped, cancellation is no longer possible but you may initiate a return upon delivery.

Delivery & Shipping
How long does delivery take?
+

Delivery times vary by location. Port Harcourt orders are typically delivered within 1–2 business days. Orders to other states in Nigeria usually take 2–5 business days depending on location and transport availability.

Do you deliver nationwide?
+

Yes, we deliver to all states in Nigeria. We use trusted transport and logistics partners to ensure your order reaches you safely. Delivery costs and timelines vary by location and will be communicated before dispatch.

How can I track my order?
+

You can track your order on our Track Order page using your order reference number. We update your order status manually as it progresses from Processing → Shipped → Delivered. You can also contact us at emmaire7@gmail.com for a direct update.

What if my order is delayed?
+

Delays can sometimes occur due to transport schedules or high demand. If your order is taking longer than expected, please contact us at emmaire7@gmail.com with your order reference and we will provide an update immediately.

Returns & Warranty
What is your return policy?
+

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items with verified factory defects or items that significantly differ from what was ordered. Items damaged by the customer, returned without original packaging, or returned after 7 days are not eligible. See our full Returns Policy for details.

Are the products covered by warranty?
+

Yes. All products sold on Nuvox are brand new and covered by the manufacturer's warranty — typically 12 months. Warranty covers manufacturing defects under normal use. Physical damage, water damage, and unauthorized modifications are not covered.

My product stopped working. What should I do?
+

Contact us at emmaire7@gmail.com with your order reference number and a description of the issue. If the item is within its 7-day return window, we will arrange a replacement or refund. If beyond 7 days but within the manufacturer warranty period, we will guide you through the warranty claim process.

Products & Stock
Are all products on Nuvox genuine and brand new?
+

Yes. We only sell 100% authentic, brand-new products sourced from authorised distributors. We do not sell refurbished, open-box, or imitation products. Every item comes in its original manufacturer packaging with all accessories included.

A product I want is out of stock. Can I be notified?
+

Yes — email us at emmaire7@gmail.com with the product name and we will notify you as soon as it is back in stock. You can also subscribe to our newsletter for first access to new arrivals and restocks.

Can I negotiate the price of a product?
+

Our prices are fixed and represent fair market value for premium, authentic products. However, we do run periodic promotions and discounts — subscribe to our newsletter or follow us to stay informed about deals.
