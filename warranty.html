Warranty Policy

Our Commitment
We Stand Behind Every Product

Every product sold on Nuvox is covered by the manufacturer's warranty. We only stock brand-new, authentic products from authorised distributors, ensuring your warranty is fully valid from the date of purchase.

In addition to manufacturer warranty, Nuvox provides direct support for any defects or issues identified within 7 days of delivery.

📱
Smartphones
12-month manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects. Does not cover physical damage, water damage, or software issues caused by third-party apps.
💻
Laptops & Computers
12-month manufacturer warranty. Battery warranty typically covers significant capacity loss. Accidental damage not included.
🎧
Audio Equipment
12-month manufacturer warranty covering hardware faults. Cable damage and physical wear are excluded from coverage.
📷
Cameras & Drones
12-month manufacturer warranty. Crash damage, water ingress without rated protection, and worn batteries are not covered.
What's Covered
Warranty Covers
Manufacturing defects present from the time of purchase
Hardware malfunctions under normal use conditions
Dead-on-arrival (DOA) devices
Screen defects not caused by physical impact
What's Not Covered
Warranty Does Not Cover
Physical damage — cracked screens, dents, broken ports
Water or liquid damage (unless the device is rated waterproof)
Damage caused by unstable power supply or incorrect voltage
Unauthorized repairs or modifications
Normal wear and tear — battery degradation, cosmetic scratches
Software issues caused by user-installed applications
